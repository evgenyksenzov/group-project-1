Dairy Free Ice Cream Recipe

Recipe is adapted from cooking.nytimes.com, written by Melissa Clark.

List of Ingredients:

  • 2 cups coconut cream
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 6 large egg yolks

Directions:

  1. In the pot, simmer the coconut cream, coconut milk, sugar and salt until sugar completely dissolves, then turn off the heat.
  2. In one bowl, whisk yolks. Slowly whisk about a third of the hot cream into the yolks, then whisk the yolk mixture back into the pot with the cream.
  3. Return pot to medium-low heat (Around 170/388 degrees) and gently cook until mixture is thick enough to coat the back of the spoon.
  4. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl.
  5. Cool mixture to room temperature, then cover and chill at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Serve :)
